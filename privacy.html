Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how TracqFitPro ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the TracqFitPro iOS application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on the features you use, we may collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information: name, email address, login credentials, and profile details.
  • Fitness and wellness data: workouts, activity metrics, body measurements, nutrition logs, hydration, and sleep entries you provide.
  • Device and app data: device model, operating system, app version, crash logs, and diagnostics.
  • Usage data: interactions with app features, session activity, and in-app preferences.

2. Health Data and Apple Health

If you choose to connect Apple Health, TracqFitPro may read and write only the health data categories you explicitly authorize in iOS permissions.

  • We access Apple Health data solely to provide app features you request.
  • We do not use Apple Health data for advertising purposes.
  • You can revoke Apple Health access at any time in iOS Settings.

3. How We Use Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide core app functionality such as tracking, analytics, and personalized training insights.
  • Maintain account security and prevent fraud or abuse.
  • Improve performance, reliability, and user experience.
  • Send service-related notifications (for example reminders, updates, or security alerts).

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only in limited circumstances:

  • With service providers that help operate the app (such as cloud hosting or analytics), under contractual confidentiality obligations.
  • When required by law, legal process, or to protect rights, safety, and security.
  • In connection with a business transfer such as merger, acquisition, or asset sale.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as needed to provide services and fulfill legal obligations. You can request deletion of your account and associated personal data as described below.

6. Your Privacy Choices

  • Access and update profile information inside the app.
  • Manage permissions (camera, notifications, Apple Health, motion) in iOS Settings.
  • Request account deletion by contacting us using the details below.

7. Data Security

We apply reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect your information. No system can be guaranteed fully secure, but we continuously work to protect your data.

8. Children's Privacy

TracqFitPro is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children without appropriate consent.

9. International Data Processing

Your information may be processed in countries other than where you live. Where applicable, we implement safeguards for cross-border data transfers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will post the revised version with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
