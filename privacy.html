Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 5, 2026

Your privacy matters. TracqFitPro is built on a simple principle: your data is yours, and yours alone.

1. No Data Collection — Ever

TracqFitPro does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data. The app developer has no access to your information of any kind.

  • No account registration required.
  • No analytics or tracking tools embedded in the app.
  • No crash reporting that sends data off your device.
  • No advertising networks or third-party SDKs that harvest data.

2. All Data Stays on Your Device

Every piece of information you enter into TracqFitPro — workouts, activity logs, body metrics, nutrition, hydration, sleep, and any other health data — is stored exclusively on your device.

  • Data never leaves your iPhone or iPad.
  • Nothing is uploaded to any server, cloud, or external service.
  • If you delete the app, all data is permanently removed from your device.

3. Apple Health

If you choose to connect Apple Health, any data read from or written to Apple Health remains under your full control via iOS Settings. TracqFitPro does not forward Apple Health data anywhere.

4. Privacy Matters

We believe privacy is a fundamental right. TracqFitPro was designed from the ground up so that you never have to trade your personal health data for app functionality. What you track is private, personal, and protected — by design, not by policy.

5. Children's Privacy

Because we collect no data whatsoever, TracqFitPro is safe for users of all ages. No personal information from any user, including children, is collected or stored by the developer.

6. Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new effective date. Our commitment to zero data collection will not change.
